Add to Home Screen

Use Memo like a native app — no browser chrome, works offline, saves to your Home Screen.

1. Open in Safari
This page must be open in Safari — not Chrome or another browser.
2. Tap the Share button
Find the Share ↑ icon in Safari's toolbar — it's at the bottom of the screen on iPhone.
3. "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the Share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap "Add"
Tap "Add" in the top-right corner to install Memo on your Home Screen.

Once installed, Memo opens full-screen with no browser bars and works fully offline.

📶 You're offline — notes are saved locally
